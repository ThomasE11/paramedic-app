# UAE Paramedic Case Generator - Student Checklist Updates Summary

## Overview
This document summarizes the updates made to student checklist items in the UAE Paramedic Case Generator. Cases with fewer than 5 checklist items were identified and expanded to have 8-12 items each.

## Summary of Changes

### cases.ts (3 cases updated)

#### 1. resp-002: Tension Pneumothorax
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Auscultate chest bilaterally for absent breath sounds
  - Percuss chest for hyperresonance on affected side
  - Assess for tracheal deviation away from affected side
  - Check for JVD and hemodynamic compromise
  - Position patient sitting upright to aid breathing
  - Monitor vital signs every 2-3 minutes
  - Pre-alert trauma center for chest drain preparation
  - Document time of decompression and patient response

#### 2. resp-003: COPD Exacerbation
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Obtain smoking history and pack-years
  - Ask about baseline oxygen requirements
  - Assess work of breathing and accessory muscle use
  - Position patient upright with tripod posture
  - Check for fever and assess sputum characteristics
  - Consider NIV/BiPAP if available and indicated
  - Transport to facility with respiratory support
  - Document oxygen delivery method and patient response

#### 3. resp-004: Pulmonary Embolism
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Obtain 12-lead ECG to assess for right heart strain
  - Assess hemodynamic stability and shock signs
  - Position patient comfortably semi-upright
  - Monitor vital signs continuously
  - Fluid resuscitation if hypotensive
  - Pre-alert receiving hospital for possible thrombolysis
  - Document risk factors and clinical probability
  - Prepare for deterioration and cardiac arrest

### additionalCases.ts (8 cases updated)

#### 4. resp-006: Pneumothorax After Chest Trauma
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Auscultate for absent breath sounds on affected side
  - Assess for tracheal deviation and JVD
  - Maintain cervical spine precautions
  - Position patient sitting upright if tolerated
  - Apply chest seal if open pneumothorax present
  - Monitor respiratory rate and effort continuously
  - Pre-alert trauma center for definitive chest tube
  - Document mechanism of injury and interventions

#### 5. resp-007: Pediatric Croup with Stridor
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Assess for signs of respiratory distress and retractions
  - Check oxygen saturation continuously
  - Ask about onset and preceding cold symptoms
  - Position child upright on parents lap
  - Differentiate from epiglottitis (drooling, toxic appearance)
  - Provide humidified oxygen if hypoxic
  - Reassure parents and explain treatment plan
  - Document stridor characteristics and interventions

#### 6. resp-009: Foreign Body Aspiration in Adult
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Assess for universal choking sign and inability to speak
  - Position hands correctly for abdominal thrusts
  - Encourage coughing if partial obstruction
  - Ensure scene safety and crowd control
  - Check airway visually if patient becomes unconscious
  - Remove visible foreign body if possible
  - Instruct bystanders to call emergency services
  - Document obstruction cause and removal method

#### 7. resp-010: Community Acquired Pneumonia
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Assess for sepsis criteria (qSOFA)
  - Auscultate for crackles and bronchial breathing
  - Ask about fever duration and sputum production
  - Monitor temperature and vital signs
  - Position patient upright for optimal ventilation
  - Antipyretics for fever management
  - Pre-alert hospital for sepsis protocol activation
  - Document CURB-65 score and antibiotic timing

#### 8. neuro-004: Bacterial Meningitis
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Assess for photophobia and neck stiffness
  - Check for non-blanching rash (glass test)
  - Monitor GCS and level of consciousness
  - Administer dexamethasone with first antibiotic dose
  - Ask about sick contacts and vaccination status
  - Assess vital signs for septic shock indicators
  - Notify infection control and public health
  - Document rash characteristics and antibiotic timing

#### 9. metab-003: Severe Hyperkalemia with ECG Changes
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Identify sine wave pattern as pre-arrest rhythm
  - Establish large-bore IV access for medication administration
  - Monitor glucose after insulin administration
  - Check for missed dialysis and nephrotoxic medications
  - Sodium bicarbonate if metabolic acidosis present
  - Continuous cardiac monitoring during treatment
  - Pre-alert receiving facility for urgent dialysis
  - Document ECG findings and medication administration times

#### 10. tox-002: Opioid Overdose with Respiratory Arrest
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Check blood glucose to rule out hypoglycemia
  - Assess respiratory rate and depth
  - Check for pinpoint pupils (miosis)
  - Titrate naloxone to respiratory effort
  - Position patient in recovery position
  - Monitor for recurrent respiratory depression
  - Prepare for post-naloxone agitation
  - Document substance use findings and naloxone response

#### 11. env-002: Heat Stroke with Altered Consciousness
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 12 checklist items
- **Added:** 8 new items
  - Obtain accurate core temperature reading
  - Assess for anhidrosis (dry hot skin)
  - Remove clothing and apply ice packs to neck, axilla, groin
  - Evaporative cooling with mist and fan
  - Monitor for seizures and administer benzodiazepines if needed
  - Aggressive IV fluid resuscitation
  - Pre-alert hospital for cooling protocol activation
  - Document temperature and cooling interventions

### enhancedCases.ts (3 cases updated)

#### 12. trauma-006: Massive Hemothorax
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 10 checklist items
- **Added:** 6 new items
  - Establish 2 large-bore IV lines (14-16G)
  - Assess for diminished breath sounds on affected side
  - Apply occlusive dressing to chest wound
  - Monitor vital signs every 2-3 minutes
  - Pre-alert trauma center for massive transfusion protocol
  - Document mechanism of injury and chest tube output

#### 13. trauma-007: Blunt Abdominal Trauma - Splenic Laceration

- **Updated:** 9 checklist items
- **Added:** 6 new items

  - Assess LUQ tenderness and guarding
  - Monitor vital signs frequently for deterioration
  - Maintain spinal precautions during examination
  - Pre-alert trauma center for surgical consultation
  - Document mechanism and abdominal exam findings

#### 14. trauma-008: Pelvic Fracture with Hemorrhagic Shock
- **Previous:** 4 checklist items
- **Updated:** 10 checklist items

  - Check for blood at urethral meatus before catheterization
  - Assess leg length discrepancy and external rotation
  - Monitor vital signs every 2-3 minutes for deterioration
  - Pre-alert trauma center for massive transfusion protocol
  - Document pelvic stability assessment and binder application
