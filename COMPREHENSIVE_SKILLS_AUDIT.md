## Protocol Quality Standards

### ✅ Comprehensive Protocol Requirements
Each comprehensive protocol includes:
- **8 structured steps** with sequential progression
- **Comprehensive keyPoints** (8-12 points per step)
- **Safety notes** for each critical step
- **Equipment requirements** clearly listed
- **Contraindications** where applicable
- **Time estimates** for each step
- **Critical step identification**

### 🔶 Partial Protocol Issues
- Missing comprehensive elements (safety notes, equipment lists)
- Incomplete step count (less than 8 steps)
- Insufficient detail in key points
